Who are we ?

We are small,beautiful,faithful 150 years classically old congregation. We like our church members.We lift each other when in need.

We love to worship our lord in our sunday morning communion services and fellowship after our morning service.

We are friends who feel like we are family and we love our friends!

What do we do ?

Every sunday morning at 09:30 AM , we gather as a small congregation and love to see and greet each other.

We proceed on to a wonderful communion service in a lutheran liturgical style(you should come see us - its beautiful).

We love to sing classical Hymns, with our Pianist - Lenny Casper and choir lead by Pastor Mariane Becker and some of us who feel like singing that particular beautiful sunday morning!
